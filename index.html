15 Rooks

The 15 rooks logic puzzle runs as follows:

If you place 15 rooks anywhere on a regular 8x8 chessboard, ensuring that every rank and every file is covered by at least one rook, then there will always be at least one rook you can remove without uncovering any rank or file. That is, at least one rook is guaranteed to be redundant, regardless of placement. This little tool is designed to visually explore the puzzle.

You can click on a square to toggle it, or click and hold to toggle many tiles at once. The counter below the board tells you how many rooks you have used, and the "highlight redundant rooks" button highlights redundant rooks if there are any. A rook is defined as redundant if removing it leaves the same number of ranks and files covered.
